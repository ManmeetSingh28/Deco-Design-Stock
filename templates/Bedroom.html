PLANNING AND DECORATING YOUR BEDROOM

How to choose the perfect bed:

When designing a bedroom,the most obvious place to start is with the bed.After all,without a good bed,it's difficult to get a good night sleep.Make sure you choose the right size for bed for the size of your bedroom.A super king size bed will dominate a small bedroom,making it difficult to move around the space or fit in any other bedroom furnitur,while a standard double bed might look a bit lost in a large bedroom.Measure your bedroom,and make sure the bed you choose will allow for at least 70cm of circulation space around the sides around the sides and end of the bed.

Decorating a bedroom-what style is best for you?
Once you've found the right size bed,it's time to choose your style.If you're looking to make a contemporary statement, an oversized headboard will make a striking focal point of your bedroom scheme. Love traditional or country style? A beautifully upholstered headboard will wok like a charm. If you're a fan of industrial chic or vintage finds,an iron bedframe might be just what you're after.

Why yor mattress is key ?
A good mattress is even more important than the bed it sits on.Take time to choose the right one for you,trying it out in-store if possible,and lying on it in various different positions.Whether you go for an innerspring mattress,memory foam mattress or anything in between,it pays to invest in well-made model. A top quality mattress should keep its shape for seven years,after which it's the optimum time to change it.Check out our guide to best mattresses.

How to choose your bedroom furniture?
A bedroom furniture set keeps things simple-it's a failsafe and often cost-effective way to create a unified bedroom scheme.But if you're feeling a bit more adventurous,start with your bed and mix and match the rest of your furniture around it.The key to any successful eclectic scheme is to choose a colour palette abd stick to it.So if you have a dark wood bed frame,match it with wooden furniture in similar tones.Similarly,if you have a white bed,choosing white or pale bedroom furniture will help the scheme to pull together as one.
